FAQ & Travel

Travel to Victoria, BC



A flight to Victoria (YYJ) is the best option.

You will likely need a rental vehicle as its ~30min from the airport into Victoria and another ~30min to Hatley Castle from there.

There are plenty of ride-share options as well (uber, taxis).

Americans:
You do NOT need a visa to enter Canada.

FAQ & Restrictions

No children (under 12)

No extras/plus-ones, we will send an invite to each guest individually.

No photo policy, this is an unplugged ceremony.
We will have a photographer and videographer at the ceremony.
If you think you can do a better job, please contact me with a quote and you can be the official photographer/videographer for this event.

There will be another ceremony in China!

This will be a mix of a traditional Chinese and Western wedding ceremony.
Primary language will be Mandarin.
Location: Qingdao or Zibo (Shandong province).
Date is pending but tentatively ~Chinese New Year 2028.

If you are interested, please let us know in the RSVP form.
A Chinese Visa is MANDATORY to enter the country.
Approximate cost will vary (especially for Americans). Alberta will be ~150CAD.

Applications owned by Google and Meta are not functional.
This includes things like:
Facebook, Instagram, Whatsapp, Google Translate, Google Chrome, Google Maps, Gmail, etc.
For communication, I recommend WeChat. This is essentially Chinese Whatsapp.
For navigation, I recommend Apple Maps (for ios) and AMap or Baidu Maps (for android).
For translation, I recommend Papago or Baidu Translate.
Most of these restrictions can be circumvented via a VPN.
